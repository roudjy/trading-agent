# QRE Operator Trust Multirun Report

The operator trust multirun report is a read-only report over governed offline
candidate batches and evidence/memory accumulation.

It answers:

- what was tested
- what passed
- what failed
- why it failed
- what evidence was missing
- what should not be retested
- what is worth testing next
- which architecture, maturity, policy, or operator-decision gates blocked
  progress

The report does not claim paper, shadow, or live readiness and grants no
strategy synthesis execution, broker, risk, order, or capital authority.
