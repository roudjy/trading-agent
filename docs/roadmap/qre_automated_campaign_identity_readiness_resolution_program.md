# ADE-QRE-022 Automated Campaign Identity and Readiness Resolution Program

## Status

Active governed follow-on program after ADE-QRE-021.

ADE-QRE-022 admits bounded, deterministic, research-only automated campaign
identity and readiness remediation through isolated generated-readiness surfaces
outside `research/**`.

## Authority

ADE-QRE-022 may:

- diagnose deterministic readiness gaps for research-registered strategies;
- retrieve identity candidates from authoritative repository artifacts;
- resolve only unique or canonically aliased identities through closed
  contracts;
- bind source, dataset, snapshot, timeframe, universe, and window capacity
  when authoritative evidence is complete;
- complete bounded generated presets, null-control execution-readiness
  assessments, campaign metadata, campaign lineage, and preregistration
  readiness reports;
- automatically admit a strategy into a preregistered campaign manifest only
  when every existing readiness gate is satisfied.

ADE-QRE-022 may not:

- guess missing identities, sources, datasets, snapshots, windows, or OOS
  independence;
- execute a campaign;
- reuse consumed OOS windows;
- claim empirical null-control completion without execution;
- grant paper, shadow, live, broker, risk, execution, or capital authority;
- write to protected `research/**` surfaces;
- modify `.claude/**`.

## Definition of Done

ADE-QRE-022 is complete only when:

- isolated generated-readiness surfaces exist outside `research/**`;
- readiness diagnoses, identity candidates, identity decisions, data bindings,
  window-capacity assessments, preset readiness, null-control execution
  readiness, campaign metadata, lineage completion, and closeout artifacts are
  persisted deterministically;
- campaign readiness remains fail-closed when any mandatory identity, data,
  window, or null-control gate is unresolved;
- only one canonical resolved research strategy catalog remains visible to
  downstream research consumers;
- no campaign execution occurs;
- tests, governance lint, architecture tests, and architecture import scan are
  green;
- frozen contracts and protected execution surfaces remain unchanged.

## Permanent Restrictions

- no `.claude/**` edits;
- no `research/**` empirical writes;
- no edits to `research/research_latest.json`;
- no edits to `research/strategy_matrix.csv`;
- no guessed identities, windows, OOS independence, costs, slippage, signal
  density, or campaign identities;
- no campaign execution;
- no paper, shadow, live, broker, risk, execution, or capital-allocation
  authority;
- no fabricated evidence or empirical null-control outcomes.
